Description

There are two major goals of the first EMOR tutorial:

  1. Installation of the required software
  2. Writing two simple programs enabling youBot to mova along two trajectories, i.e. a circle and a square
During the first meeting the students can get grades for:
  1. Cloning emor_trs git repository (0.5p)
  2. Installation of V-Rep (0.5p)
  3. Preparation of Matlab V-Rep bindings (0.5p)
  4. Installation of RVC toolbox for Matlab (0.5p)
  5. Making the abovedescribed software packages to work together (1p)
The description the installation procedure can be found at setup website. Whereas their homework is to :
  1. Generation of a circular trajectory (2p)
  2. Generation of a "square trajectory" (2p)
In both cases they should write program on the basis of the exercise01.m file, by modifying the section between the % EMOR exercise 01 begin and % EMOR exercise end comments.

Following of a circle trajectory

The first trajectory the youBot robot has to follow is a circle.

Following of a square trajectory

The second trajectory is four egdes of a square. You can control one of the youBot that can move around a virtual scene. You need to provide the youBot with a program that allows it to move along egdes of a square and visit all vertices. When the robot reaches the first vertex, it stops.

The lenght of the edge of the square should be 1m.
